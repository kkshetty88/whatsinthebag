Host a Game

• You can host a new game by clicking the "Start a new game" button.
• Enter your name and wait for the new game id to be created.
• Share the game id with your friends and wait for them to join.
• Click "Create Teams" to assign teams to each player. Click "Submit Teams" once assigned.
• You can start the game by pressing "Begin". The host of the game will be the first one to start.

Join a Game

• You can enter a game by clicking the "Join a game" button.
• Enter your name and the game Id shared by the host.
• Wait for the host to create the teams.
• When it is your turn, press the "Resume" button to begin your turn.

Basic Game Rules

• This game consists of two teams, mainly Team A and Team B.
• We begin the game with 40 random words and use these words for 3 rounds.
• The main idea behind the game is for your team members to guess more words than the other team.

Round 1

• The game begins with Round 1 where the host (from Team A) sees the first word and tries to describe the word to his team using a sentence/synonym/antonym etc.
For eg. Lets say the word is "snowflake", you could say "Frozen ice that falls from the sky". You cannot say the word snow in your clue.
• If your team member guesses it correctly you mark the "Correct" button. If not, you could either "Pass" or keep giving more clues.
• If its guessed correctly your team earns 1 point and you move on to the next word and so on. You will get 1 minute to describe as many words as possible.
• Once the time's up, the player from Team B, continues the game by hitting the "Resume" button on his/her screen.
• This is continued till all the 40 words are over. Once complete we move to Round 2.

Round 2

• We use the same set of 40 words but use a one word clue. The next player in line gets to start this round.
For eg. Lets say the word is "snowflake" again, you could say "Ice". If all the players had noted snowflake from the previous round, they can guess it correctly.
• If your team member guesses it correctly you mark the "Correct" button. If not, you could "Pass" or wait till the time's up
• If its guessed correctly your team earns 1 point and you move on to the next word and so on. You will get 1 minute to give one word clues to as many words as possible.
• Once the time's up, the next player in line continues the game by hitting the "Resume" button on his/her screen.
• This is continued till all the 40 words are over. Once complete we move to Round 3.

Round 3

• We use the same set of 40 words but use dumb charades.
• If your team member guesses it correctly you mark the "Correct" button. If not, you could "Pass" or wait till the time's up
• If its guessed correctly your team earns 1 point and you move on to the next word and so on. You will get 1 minute to act out as many words as possible.
• Once the time's up, the next player in line continues the game by hitting the "Resume" button on his/her screen.
• This is continued till all the 40 words are over. Once complete, the Winner is declared.

Pass

• Each player gets one word he can pass/skip in every turn.
• The passed word is reshuffled back in the deck.

Switch Next Turn

• This button should be used only in scenarios where Team A gets advantage over Team B and we would want the next round to be started by Team B instead.
